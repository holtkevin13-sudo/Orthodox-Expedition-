Adding prizes to each category
Once your family account is approved you will set up your prize shop from your parent dashboard. For each category enter the prize name, an emoji, and the coin cost — the dollar value calculates automatically. Common prizes should fall in your chosen Common range, and so on. You can add as many prizes as you like and toggle them on or off at any time.
